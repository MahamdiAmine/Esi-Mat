Makerspace

A student built, student run, makerspace

Venture Factory (BYU's Product Development Accelerator club) and BYU Developers Club co-founded a collaborative space for students across campus. Our goal is to facilitate innovation. In the Makerspace, students have free access to several computers, IoT devices, three 3-D printers (including filament), and much more.

This has been made possible through over $20,000 in grants from the Ira A. Fulton College of Engineering and Technology, $20,000 in gifts from Microsoft, club leadership and membership, and our exceptional club advisors (respectively Professors Mike Miles and Justin Zsiros, and Professor Christophe Giraud-Carrier).
